How to use

  1. Choose what you want to be tested on using the checkboxes.
  2. Type your answers in the input boxes.
  3. Click Submit to check your answers and update your score.
  4. Click Next to get a new species.
  5. Use Show images to turn images on/off (for harder studying, turn images off).
  6. If having trouble with image try clicking images off then back on.
