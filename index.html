How to use

  1. Pick your animal group filters (left sidebar) to study only those right now.
  2. Choose what you want to be tested on using the checkboxes (left sidebar).
  3. Type your answers in the input boxes.
  4. Click Submit to check your answers and update your score.
  5. Click Next to get a new species.
  6. Use Show images to turn images on/off (harder studying: turn off) (Note:The images are not always accurate since they come from Wikipedia so use this function with caution.)
  7. If you enable hints, they auto-reveal like flashcards.

Good luck! - Anna :)
